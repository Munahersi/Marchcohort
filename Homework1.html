Homemade lemoncake

what is your fav cake? mine is lemon cake?

INGREDIENTS

  • eggs>
  • thich skinned lemons
  • castor suger

how to make lemoncake
  1. Step 1: Heat the oven to 180/160 fan/ gas 4
  2. Step 2: Beat together the butter and castor suger together until pale and creamy, then add the eggs one a a time
  3. Step 3: Sift in the self-raising flour, then add the lemon zest and mix untill well combined
  4. Step 4: Line a loaf tin (8 x 21cm) with greaseproof paper, then spoo in the mixure and level the top with a spoon
  5. Step 5: Bake for 45-50 mins untill a thin skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean
  6. Step 6: While the cake is cooling in its tin, mix together the lemon juice and caster sugar to make the drizzle
  7. Step 7: Prick the warm cake all over with a skewer or fork, then pour over the drizzle
